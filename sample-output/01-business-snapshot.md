# Business Snapshot — Fluxen
*Go2Rev GTM Planner · Step 1 of 5 · (sample output, fictional company)*

## 1. Business context
**Sector:** energy-management SaaS (EMS) for manufacturing. **Size:** €900k
ARR, 11 employees, founded 2021, Valencia (ES). **Model:** annual SaaS
subscriptions (€12–30k/plant/year) + paid onboarding. **Moment:** post
product-market signal (14 plants live, 92% retention) but growth stalled at
founder-network exhaustion. **Geography:** Spain; Portugal opportunistic.

## 2. Current offer and value proposition
**What they sell:** real-time energy monitoring + anomaly alerts + reporting
for ISO 50001 audits, installed on existing meters (no new hardware).
**Who actually buys:** mid-size manufacturers (50–500 employees) — food
processing, plastics, automotive components. **Why customers say they
choose them:** setup in days, not months; reports auditors accept.
**Perceived differentiators:** "easiest EMS to deploy" — flagged
`assumption` (low confidence): no win/loss data behind it.

## 3. Target segments and ICP
**Primary:** mid-size manufacturing plants in energy-intensive processes,
50–500 employees, with ISO 50001 certification or pursuing it, no dedicated
energy manager. **Decision-maker:** plant manager or operations director;
cares about energy cost line, audit burden, production continuity.
**Influencer:** quality/compliance manager (owns ISO). **Qualification
signals:** energy spend >€250k/year; certification deadline in 6–12 months.
**Secondary:** industrial groups standardizing across 3–10 plants.

## 4. Current funnel and sales cycle
**Lead sources today:** founder network (~60%), referrals from installed
plants (~25%), inbound trickle (~15%) — splits are user estimates, no CRM
export. **Sales cycle:** 2–4 months. **Close rate:** "about 1 in 4
proposals" — `assumption`, medium confidence. **Average deal:** €18k/year.
~20 real opportunities last 12 months.

## 5. Stack and tools
Pipedrive (lightly used, no source tracking), Mailchimp (dormant),
WordPress site. No marketing automation.

## 6. Revenue team
Founder/CEO sells; CTO supports technical demos; 1 CS person onboards. No
marketing hire. **Founder dependence: high** — every deal touches the CEO.

## 7. Stated goals
8 new plants in 12 months (≈ +€150k ARR), reducing founder dependence on
origination. Success metric: signed plants + first pipeline not originated
by the founder.

## 8. Known constraints
Marketing budget ≤ €4k/month. No one to run campaigns full-time. Sales
capacity: founder can handle ~4 active opportunities in parallel.

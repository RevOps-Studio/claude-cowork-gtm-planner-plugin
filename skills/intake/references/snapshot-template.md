# Business Snapshot — Template

Produce the snapshot with exactly this structure. Do not add or remove
sections; a section without data carries its open question instead.

---

# Business Snapshot — [Company]
*Go2Rev GTM Planner · Step 1 of 5 · [date]*

## 1. Business context
**Sector and category:** — **Size and maturity:** (revenue range, years,
headcount) — **Business model:** (how revenue is generated: direct sales /
channel / recurring / projects) — **Current moment:** (why they are building
a GTM plan now) — **Geography:** (current and target markets)

## 2. Current offer and value proposition
**What they sell** · **Who actually buys today** (the real customer, not the
aspirational ICP) · **Why customers say they choose them** · **Perceived
differentiators** (what they believe sets them apart — flag with an
`assumption` open question if unverified)

## 3. Target segments and ICP
**Primary segment** · **Decision-maker profile** (role, responsibilities, top
concerns) · **Influencer profile** · **Qualification signals** (how they know
a lead is worth pursuing) · **Secondary segments** (brief)

## 4. Current funnel and sales cycle
**How leads arrive today** (sources, with % split if known) · **Current
qualification process** · **Stages of the sales process** · **Average sales
cycle** · **Estimated close rate** · **Average deal size**

## 5. Stack and tools
**CRM** (tool, usage level, data quality) · **Marketing** (email, automation,
analytics, CMS) · **Reporting** (how results are measured today)

## 6. Revenue team
**Sales structure** · **Marketing structure** · **Ops/RevOps** (who owns CRM,
data, process — if anyone) · **Founder dependence** (how much of selling
still runs through the founder)

## 7. Stated goals
**Primary goal** · **Time horizon** · **Success metrics as the user defines
them**

## 8. Known constraints
**Budget** (range) · **Team capacity** · **Technology** · **Time** ·
**Other** (governance, politics)

## 9. Open questions
List every `missing-data`, `assumption` and `conflict` item raised during
intake, each with its recommendation. These travel with the plan until
resolved.

---

## Snapshot confidence
Two or three sentences: what is solid, what is thin, and the single most
valuable input the user could still provide. No numeric scores.
